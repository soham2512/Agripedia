The PDMS

( Plant Disease Monitoring System )


PDMS is a online portal system that detects the Disease from the image provided by the farmer.
It also provides a whole guide to the farmers that how to get the disease in control by providing list of medicines.
The system also provides with the suggestions that how a crop get infected and possible solutions to overcome similar future problems. THus helps the farmer to gain maximum output from their farming by detecting the disease and also providing solutions to it.
